Phase 0: the beacon chain

There are many great and primary explainers about the beacon chain and I pick one for you.

https://ethereum.org/en/eth2/the-beacon-chain/

Just read it. :)

In this article, I don’t want to explain too further about the beacon chain cause if I do that, the shard chains must put on the table and I have to introduce shards, slots, epochs, validations, attestations, crosslinks, committees, checkpoints, finality, staking rewards and penalties one by one. As you can see, we solely introduce the beacon chain in phase 0 but not the shard chains which will be shipped in phase 1. That’s so awkward!

Currently, just remember the beacon chain’s main role in ETH 2.0 is making sure all the shards have the most up-to-date data and the beacon chain will be responsible for registering validators and coordinating everyone’s staked ETH in phase 0.
